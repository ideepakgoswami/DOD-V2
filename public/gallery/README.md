# 📸 Gallery Photos Folder

Drop your own photos into the subfolders below. The gallery page will automatically pick them up!

## Folder Structure

```
public/gallery/
├── events/         → Photos from events, weddings, annual days, festivals
├── performance/    → Stage performances, solo & group dance shows
├── fitness/        → Zumba, aerobics, yoga, fitness classes
├── academy/        → Kids, teen & adult training sessions
└── production/     → Music video shoots, behind-the-scenes
```

## How to Add Photos

1. Choose the right category subfolder
2. Copy your `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, or `.webp` photo into it
3. The filename becomes the image title (underscores/hyphens become spaces)
   - Example: `Wedding_Sangeet_Performance.jpg` → "Wedding Sangeet Performance"
4. Refresh the gallery page — your photo appears automatically!

## Supported Formats
- `.jpg` / `.jpeg`
- `.png`
- `.webp`

## Tips
- Recommended size: 800×600px or larger (landscape or square works best)
- Keep filenames descriptive, e.g. `Kids_Summer_Camp_2024.jpg`
- If a folder is empty, that category won't show any images
